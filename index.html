FUT Champions

FUT Champs, for those still using the FIFA terminology - is the most competitive
(and rage-inducing) mode in Ultimate Team, but with the best rewards, so you can go out and buy the latest and greatest card

Squad Battels

Squad Battles is an Ultimate Team™ mode where you take on squads controlled by the central processing unit (CPU) from the Ultimate Team™ community to earn Battle Points, move up the leaderboards, and earn rewards

Rivals

Rivals is an online game mode within FC 24 Ultimate Team that rewards with packs,
coins and Champions points everyone
who has won at least three match in the previous week.

Friendly

Friendlies is a game mode within Ultimate Team that allows you to play online or with friends. It is a fun and relaxed way to play FC 24. Play locally with your friends, or against the AI, from your couch.

Online Cup

A knockout tournament consisting
of a round of 16 matches, a quarter-final,
semi-final, and final.
You get one online play-off match
for each round.
